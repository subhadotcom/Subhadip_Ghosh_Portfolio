About Me

Get to know more about my background and expertise

I am a passionate Data Analyst skilled in Python, SQL, HTML, and CSS, with hands-on experience in data analysis and visualization using Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly. I work with databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and SQLite to extract and transform data for actionable insights.

I have delivered projects leveraging Streamlit for interactive dashboards and automated workflows, focusing on clear data storytelling and practical business impact. My approach combines technical expertise with a strong understanding of analytics, enabling effective communication of results to stakeholders.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new data technologies, contributing to open-source analytics tools, and sharing knowledge through blogs and community events.
